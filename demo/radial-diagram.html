Multi-line labels

\n produces a line break in center.label, segment names, and facet names. Centre hub and facet labels stack vertically; segment labels stack along the radius of the label band, each line on its own curved arc.

Inner vs outer label position

style.segmentLabelPosition controls where the dimension labels sit:

  • "outer" (default) — the labelled colour band sits outside the wheel. Best for assessments where the wedge area visualises scoring.
  • "inner" — the labelled band sits between the centre hub and the facet area, with ring dividers on both edges. Best for framework-style diagrams. Note: the inner band visually overlays the inner part of the wedge, so partial score fills may be hidden behind it.
